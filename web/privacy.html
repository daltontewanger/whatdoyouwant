Your Privacy Matters

This privacy policy describes how the What Do You Want?! app (“the App”) handles your information.

Information Collected

  • Location Data: The App requests access to your device location only while using the app in order to show you nearby restaurants and places to eat.

No other personal data is collected, stored, or shared. The app does not require registration or account creation, and does not track users.

How Location Data is Used

  • Your current location is used only to display options for nearby restaurants.
  • No location history is stored, transmitted, or retained after your session ends.

Third-Party Services

  • The App may use map or restaurant search providers (such as HERE API or Google Places) to show nearby places, but your location is sent only for finding results and is never linked to your identity.

Data Security

  • The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information beyond your current location for the purpose of searching.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted here with the “Last updated” date.
